Cheshire Cat

About Cheshire Cat

The Cheshire Cat is the cat of the Duchess. Mischievous and unpredictable in nature, the Cheshire Cat acts as a guide for Alice during her adventures in Wonderland, but also takes pleasure in causing her trouble.

Cheshire Cat's Characteristics

  1. Mysterious, pink-and-purple-striped cat with a permanent grin.
  2. Constantly grins and can disappear and reappear whenever it likes.
  3. Sometimes it disappears and leaves its grin behind.
